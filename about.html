About The School

This is an arabic and islamic institution founded on the basis of Al-islaam, and to cater for the grooming of Islaamic Scholars. The muas-sis of this amiable insitute, Ash-shaykh Miftaahuddeen ibn 'Abdul-kareem ibn Roojii, has done a great a job by deserting his full time into teaching Arabic and Basic principles of Islaam. And also, Perfect Recitation of the Glorious Qur-aan is the madrasah's first agenda with understanding of Basic Usuulut-tajweed.
