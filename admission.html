Madrasah's admission form

Do you want to be part of Al-furqoon? If yes, here is the way to apply
  1. Click on proceed and fill the form.
  2. Then, press the submit button, and wait while the page loads.
  3. Make sure the input data is/are correct.
  4. Printing of form is required for reference purpose, and payment of requested charges before printing the filled form.
  5. After completing the registration, kndly visit the Madrasah @ No. 17, Oluwakemi Mosque, Olodo, Sabo, Sagamu, Ogun state.
